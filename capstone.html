Business Analytics Consulting Capstone Project

Canadian Cell Phone Plan Analysis

This project was completed as part of my final semester at Seneca College. It analyzed Canadian cell phone plans compared to U.S. offerings, with the goal of providing a data-driven assessment of how Canadian plans perform in terms of value, features, and cost. Our team developed a scoring model that incorporated both qualitative and quantitative data.

What I contributed

  • Identified key columns from the dataset (Table 3) and cleaned and normalized the data
  • Defined the core features to assess (e.g. data, coverage, extras)
  • Designed the scoring model and normalization method
  • Assigned importance weights based on appendix data
  • Developed metrics like points per dollar for comparison
  • Created visualizations and comparative graphs
  • Drew final insights and supported the report narrative
